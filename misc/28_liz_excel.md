## Event
Take your Microsoft Excel skills to the next level with this webinar on data analysis. Participants will learn the key steps in exploratory data analysis, get exposed to handy ways to clean and summarize data from NYC city agencies, and explore best practices for making charts, maps and other visualizations.

## About the Speaker
Elizabeth DiLuzio specializes in organizational strategy, program evaluation, and adult learning. She has a history of facilitating organizational development of evaluation processes and data-informed cultural change. Her work is rooted in participatory methodology and a strengths-based approach. Elizabeth is a PhD candidate at the University of New Hampshire.
